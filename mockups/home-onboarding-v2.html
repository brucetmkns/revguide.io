Design Comparison

Option A: Timeline

  • Best for linear step-by-step flows
  • Progress ring provides at-a-glance status
  • Timeline shows clear path forward
  • Compact stats in hero section
  • Most similar to current, improved visual hierarchy

Option B: Kanban

  • Best for users familiar with project boards
  • Shows all tasks in context at once
  • Clear done/current/todo separation
  • May feel busy for simple onboarding
  • Good for power users

Option C: Grouped

  • Best for longer onboarding flows
  • Groups related tasks together
  • Collapsible sections reduce overwhelm
  • Progress bar is simple and clear
  • Expandable for more detail when needed
