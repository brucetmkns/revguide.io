How to Create Plays (Battle Cards)

Build comprehensive guides for sales scenarios and processes

Plays (also known as Battle Cards) are detailed reference cards that provide your team with structured guidance for specific sales scenarios, competitor comparisons, objection handling, and more. They appear in the RevGuide side panel when users are working in HubSpot.

What Are Plays?

Plays are versatile content cards that can include:

  • Competitor battle cards with positioning and differentiation
  • Sales playbooks for specific deal stages
  • Objection handling scripts and responses
  • Process checklists and workflows
  • Product feature guides
  • Pricing and negotiation guidelines

Creating a Play

1 Navigate to Plays

Open the RevGuide admin panel and click "Plays" in the left sidebar.

2 Click Add Play

Click the "Add Play" button. A new play editor will open.

3 Enter Play Details
  • Title: Name your play (e.g., "Competitor X Battle Card")
  • Category: Select a category to organize your plays
  • Description: Brief summary of what this play covers
4 Add Content

Use the rich text editor to add your play content. You can include:

  • Formatted text with headings, bold, and italics
  • Bullet points and numbered lists
  • Links to resources
  • Embedded videos
  • Tables for comparisons
5 Set Display Conditions (Optional)

Configure when this play should be highlighted or appear in the side panel based on URL patterns.

6 Save Your Play

Click "Save" to create your play. It will be immediately available in the side panel.

Organize your plays into categories for easier navigation:

Battle Card Best Practices

For Competitor Battle Cards

  • Overview: Brief description of the competitor
  • Strengths: What they do well (be honest)
  • Weaknesses: Where they fall short
  • Our Differentiation: Why customers choose you
  • Common Objections: What prospects say and how to respond
  • Landmines: Questions to ask that highlight your advantages
  • Win Stories: Examples of competitive wins

For Process Guides

  • Use numbered steps for sequential processes
  • Include links to relevant tools or resources
  • Add screenshots where helpful
  • Keep content scannable with headers and bullets
Tip: Keep plays focused on a single topic. It's better to have multiple specific plays than one long, generic document.

Accessing Plays in HubSpot

Users can access plays through the RevGuide side panel:

  1. Click the RevGuide icon in the HubSpot toolbar
  2. Navigate to the "Plays" tab in the side panel
  3. Browse by category or search for specific plays
  4. Click a play to expand and view the full content

Managing Plays

Organizing with Categories

Use consistent categories across your plays. Users can filter plays by category in the side panel.

Updating Content

Keep plays current by reviewing and updating them regularly. Outdated information can be worse than no information.

Enable/Disable

Temporarily disable plays that need updates without deleting them.

Note: Plays are shared across your entire organization. All team members will see the same plays in their side panel.

Play Templates

Consider creating these common play types:

  • New Hire Onboarding: Essential information for new team members
  • Deal Stage Checklists: What to do at each pipeline stage
  • Discovery Questions: Key questions to ask prospects
  • Demo Best Practices: Tips for effective product demos
  • Pricing Conversations: How to discuss pricing and negotiate
