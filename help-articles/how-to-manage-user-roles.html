How to Manage User Roles

Understand and configure permissions for your team

RevGuide uses a role-based access control system to manage what team members can see and do. Understanding roles helps you set up appropriate permissions and maintain security.

Available Roles

Owner

The highest level of access. Each organization has exactly one owner.

  • Full administrative access
  • Can transfer ownership to another admin
  • Cannot be removed by other admins
  • Responsible for billing (when applicable)

Admin

Full administrative access except ownership functions.

  • Manage team members (invite, remove, change roles)
  • Connect and disconnect HubSpot
  • Update organization settings
  • Create, edit, and delete all content
  • Import and export data

Editor

Content management without administrative access.

  • Create, edit, and delete banners, plays, and wiki entries
  • Import fields from HubSpot
  • Export organization data
  • Cannot manage team members or settings

Viewer

Read-only access to all content.

  • View all banners, plays, and wiki entries
  • Use the RevGuide side panel in HubSpot
  • See wiki tooltips throughout HubSpot
  • Cannot create, edit, or delete any content

Choosing the Right Role

Assign Viewer When:

  • The person only needs to consume content, not create it
  • They're new to the team and learning the system
  • They're in a role that doesn't require content management

Assign Editor When:

  • The person will create and maintain content
  • They need to import fields or update wiki entries
  • They're a content contributor but not a team manager

Assign Admin When:

  • The person needs to manage team members
  • They're responsible for HubSpot integration
  • They're a team lead or manager
  • You need backup administrative coverage
Tip: Follow the principle of least privilege. Give users the minimum role needed for their job. You can always upgrade roles later.

Role Indicators

User roles are displayed throughout RevGuide:

  • Sidebar: Your role appears next to your name
  • Team Table: Each member's role is shown as a badge
  • Page Restrictions: View-only users see a badge on restricted pages

What Viewers See

When a viewer accesses RevGuide:

  • Edit buttons are hidden on all content pages
  • Add/Create buttons are hidden
  • Delete and duplicate functions are unavailable
  • A "View Only" badge appears on content pages
  • The Team Members section is hidden in Settings

Transferring Ownership

Only the current owner can transfer ownership:

1 Promote to Admin First

The person receiving ownership must first be an Admin. Change their role if needed.

2 Contact Support

For security reasons, ownership transfers require contacting RevGuide support.

Warning: Ownership transfer is permanent. The current owner becomes an Admin after transfer. Make sure you're transferring to the right person.

Best Practices

Multiple Admins

Have at least two admins in your organization. This ensures:

  • Coverage when one admin is unavailable
  • Ability to manage the team if someone leaves
  • Shared responsibility for administrative tasks

Regular Audits

Periodically review team roles:

  • Remove users who have left the company
  • Downgrade roles for users who no longer need them
  • Upgrade roles for users who need additional access

Documentation

Document who has admin access and why. This helps with:

  • Security audits
  • Onboarding new team leads
  • Understanding permission history
Note: Role changes take effect immediately. The user may need to refresh their browser to see updated permissions.
