How to Install RevGuide

Get started with RevGuide in just a few minutes

RevGuide is a Chrome extension that enhances your HubSpot experience with contextual banners, plays, and wiki tooltips. Follow these steps to install and set up RevGuide.

Installation Steps

1 Install the Chrome Extension

Visit the Chrome Web Store and search for "RevGuide" or use the direct link provided by your administrator. Click "Add to Chrome" to install the extension.

2 Create Your Account

After installation, click the RevGuide icon in your browser toolbar. You'll be prompted to create an account or sign in. Enter your email address and create a password, or sign in with your existing credentials.

3 Set Up Your Organization

During signup, you'll provide your name and company name. This creates your organization workspace where you can manage content and invite team members.

4 Connect to HubSpot (Optional)

For full functionality including field import and property-based conditions, connect your HubSpot portal. Go to Settings and click "Connect HubSpot" to authorize the connection.

5 Start Creating Content

Navigate to the Admin Panel to create your first banner, play, or wiki entry. Your content will automatically appear when you're working in HubSpot.

System Requirements

  • Google Chrome browser (version 88 or later)
  • Active HubSpot account
  • Internet connection
Tip: Pin the RevGuide extension icon to your Chrome toolbar for quick access. Right-click the extension icon and select "Pin" from the menu.

Web App Access

You can also access RevGuide directly at app.supered.io without the Chrome extension. The web app provides the full admin experience for managing your content, while the extension enables in-HubSpot features like tooltips and banners.

Note: If you received an invitation from a team member, click the invitation link in your email to join their organization instead of creating a new one.
