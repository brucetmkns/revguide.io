How to Create Your First Banner

Display contextual messages and guidance in HubSpot

Banners are dynamic messages that appear at the top or bottom of HubSpot pages. Use them to share announcements, process reminders, training tips, or any contextual information your team needs.

Creating a Banner

1 Navigate to Banners

Open the RevGuide admin panel and click "Banners" in the left sidebar.

2 Click Add Banner

Click the "Add Banner" button in the top right corner. A new banner form will appear.

3 Enter Banner Details

Fill in the following fields:

  • Title: A descriptive name for your banner (internal use only)
  • Message: The text that will display to users. You can use rich text formatting including bold, italic, links, and lists.
4 Choose Banner Style

Select a style that matches your message intent:

  • Info (Blue): General information and tips
  • Success (Green): Positive updates or confirmations
  • Warning (Yellow): Cautions or important reminders
  • Error (Red): Critical alerts or urgent messages
5 Set Display Conditions

Define when and where your banner appears. See the conditions section below for details.

6 Save Your Banner

Click "Save" to create your banner. It will be enabled by default and start appearing in HubSpot immediately.

Display Conditions

Banners can be targeted to specific pages or contexts using URL patterns:

Pattern Description Example
/contacts/* All contact pages Contact records, lists
/deals/* All deal pages Deal records, pipelines
/companies/* All company pages Company records
/tickets/* All ticket pages Ticket records
* All HubSpot pages Global announcement

Banner Position

By default, banners appear at the top of the page. You can change the position in Settings:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Find "Display Options"
  3. Change "Banner Position" to Top or Bottom

Adding Media to Banners

Banners can include embedded content:

  • Videos: Embed Loom, YouTube, or Vimeo videos
  • Images: Add screenshots or diagrams
  • Links: Include links to documentation or resources
Tip: Keep banner messages concise. For longer content, use a link to full documentation or consider creating a Play instead.

Managing Banners

Enable/Disable

Toggle a banner on or off without deleting it. Click the status indicator on the banner card to toggle.

Edit

Click on any banner in the list to open it for editing. Make your changes and click Save.

Duplicate

Create a copy of an existing banner using the duplicate button. This is useful for creating similar banners with different conditions.

Delete

Click the delete button on a banner card to remove it permanently.

Note: Banners are visible to all users in your organization. Use display conditions to target specific audiences based on the pages they visit.

Best Practices

  • Use descriptive titles so you can easily identify banners later
  • Choose appropriate styles - don't overuse warning/error for non-urgent messages
  • Keep messages actionable - tell users what to do, not just what to know
  • Set specific URL patterns to avoid banner fatigue
  • Review and update banners regularly to keep content fresh
