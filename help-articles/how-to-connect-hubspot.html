How to Connect HubSpot

Enable advanced features by connecting your HubSpot portal

Connecting RevGuide to your HubSpot portal unlocks powerful features like importing HubSpot fields into your wiki, using property-based display conditions, and more.

Why Connect HubSpot?

  • Import Fields: Automatically import HubSpot property definitions into your wiki
  • Property Groups: Organize wiki entries by HubSpot property groups
  • Smart Conditions: Display banners and plays based on HubSpot object types
  • Field Documentation: Include field types, options, and API names in wiki entries

Connection Steps

1 Navigate to Settings

Open the RevGuide admin panel and click on "Settings" in the left sidebar.

2 Find the HubSpot Connection Section

Scroll to the "HubSpot Connection" card. You'll see either a "Connect HubSpot" button or your current connection status.

3 Click Connect HubSpot

Click the "Connect HubSpot" button. You'll be redirected to HubSpot's authorization page.

4 Authorize RevGuide

On HubSpot's page, select the portal you want to connect and review the requested permissions. Click "Connect app" to authorize RevGuide.

5 Confirmation

You'll be redirected back to RevGuide Settings. A success message will confirm your portal is connected, showing your portal name and domain.

Required Permissions

RevGuide requests the following HubSpot permissions:

  • CRM Schemas: Read access to understand your custom properties
  • Properties: Read access to import field definitions
Tip: RevGuide only reads property metadata. It does not access, modify, or store your actual CRM data or contacts.

Disconnecting HubSpot

To disconnect your HubSpot portal:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. In the HubSpot Connection card, click "Disconnect"
  3. Confirm the disconnection when prompted
Warning: Disconnecting HubSpot will disable field import and property-based features. Your existing wiki entries will remain but won't be updated from HubSpot.

Troubleshooting

  • Connection Failed: Ensure you have admin or super admin access in your HubSpot portal
  • Portal Not Listed: You can only connect portals where you have appropriate permissions
  • Authorization Error: Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then retry the connection
Note: Only organization admins and owners can connect or disconnect HubSpot. Team members with viewer or editor roles cannot modify the HubSpot connection.
