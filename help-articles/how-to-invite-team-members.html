How to Invite Team Members

Add your team to RevGuide and collaborate on content

RevGuide is designed for team collaboration. Invite your colleagues to share banners, plays, and wiki entries across your organization. Each team member can access the same content when working in HubSpot.

Prerequisites

  • You must be an Admin or Owner to invite new team members
  • Team members need a valid email address

Inviting a Team Member

1 Navigate to Settings

Open the RevGuide admin panel and click "Settings" in the left sidebar.

2 Find Team Members Section

Scroll down to the "Team Members" card. You'll see a list of current members and pending invitations.

3 Click Invite User

Click the "Invite User" button. An invitation modal will appear.

4 Enter Email Address

Type the email address of the person you want to invite.

5 Select Role

Choose the appropriate role for this team member:

  • Viewer: Can view all content but cannot edit
  • Editor: Can view and edit banners, plays, and wiki entries
  • Admin: Full access including team management and settings
6 Send Invitation

Click "Send Invite". The person will receive an email with a link to join your organization.

What Happens After Sending

  1. The invitee receives an email with an invitation link
  2. They click the link and create their RevGuide account (or sign in if they have one)
  3. They're automatically added to your organization with the assigned role
  4. They can immediately access your shared content

Managing Invitations

Pending Invitations

Invitations that haven't been accepted appear in the Team Members table with a "Pending" status.

Resending Invitations

If someone didn't receive their invitation email:

  1. Find their pending invitation in the table
  2. Click the resend icon (envelope/send icon)
  3. A new invitation email will be sent

Canceling Invitations

To cancel a pending invitation:

  1. Find the pending invitation
  2. Click the trash/delete icon
  3. Confirm the cancellation
Note: Cancelled invitations can no longer be used to join. You'll need to send a new invitation if you want that person to join later.

Tip: Start with the Viewer role for new team members. You can always upgrade their role later once they're familiar with RevGuide.

Removing Team Members

To remove someone from your organization:

  1. Go to Settings > Team Members
  2. Find the team member you want to remove
  3. Click the trash/delete icon
  4. Confirm the removal

Removed members:

  • Immediately lose access to your organization's content
  • Cannot see banners, plays, or wiki entries from your organization
  • Can still use RevGuide if they create or join another organization
Note: You cannot remove yourself from the organization. Owners must transfer ownership before leaving.

Best Practices

  • Role Assignment: Give users the minimum permissions they need
  • Onboarding: Share this help documentation with new team members
  • Regular Review: Periodically review team members and remove inactive users
  • Multiple Admins: Consider having at least 2 admins for redundancy

Troubleshooting

Invitation Email Not Received

  • Check spam/junk folders
  • Verify the email address is correct
  • Try resending the invitation
  • Ask IT if there are email filters blocking RevGuide

User Already Exists Error

This means the email is already associated with a RevGuide account. The person should:

  1. Sign in to their existing RevGuide account
  2. Accept the invitation from their inbox
