How to Use the Side Panel

Access plays, wiki, and resources while working in HubSpot

The RevGuide side panel is your command center for accessing plays, wiki entries, and helpful resources without leaving your HubSpot workflow. It slides out from the right side of the screen, keeping your content just a click away.

Opening the Side Panel

1 Find the RevGuide Icon

Look for the RevGuide icon in the HubSpot interface. It appears in the toolbar or floating button area.

2 Click to Open

Click the icon to slide open the side panel. It appears on the right side of your screen.

3 Close When Done

Click the X button or the RevGuide icon again to close the panel.

Side Panel Tabs

The side panel organizes content into tabs:

Tab Content Use When
Plays Battle cards and guides Need detailed playbooks or competitor info
Wiki Term definitions Looking up acronyms or field definitions
Media Videos and presentations Need training videos or demos

Using the Plays Tab

Browsing Plays

  • Plays are organized by category
  • Click a category to expand/collapse it
  • Click a play title to view its content
  • Click again to collapse

Searching Plays

  • Use the search box at the top of the tab
  • Search matches play titles and content
  • Results update as you type

Quick Access

Frequently used plays appear at the top. The panel remembers your recently viewed plays for quick access.

Tip: During a sales call, keep the side panel open to the Plays tab. You can quickly reference competitor battle cards or objection handling guides.

Using the Wiki Tab

Searching Definitions

  • Type a term in the search box
  • Matching entries appear as you type
  • Click an entry to view its full definition

Browsing by Category

Wiki entries are grouped by:

  • Object type (Contacts, Companies, Deals, Tickets)
  • Property group (from HubSpot)
  • Custom categories

Tooltip Integration

The same definitions that appear as tooltips in HubSpot are accessible here. Use the side panel for a more detailed view or to browse related terms.

Using the Media Tab

Viewing Videos

  • Click a video to expand and play
  • Videos play directly in the side panel
  • Supports Loom, YouTube, and Vimeo

Training Content

The Media tab is ideal for:

  • Product demo videos
  • Process walkthroughs
  • Training recordings
  • Feature tutorials

Side Panel Features

Resizing

Drag the left edge of the panel to resize it wider or narrower.

Persistent State

The panel remembers:

  • Your last open tab
  • Recent searches
  • Panel width preference

Links and Actions

  • Links in plays open in new browser tabs
  • Videos play inline without leaving HubSpot
  • Admin link (if visible) opens the RevGuide admin panel

Best Practices

During Sales Calls

  1. Open the side panel before the call
  2. Navigate to relevant plays (competitor, objections)
  3. Keep it ready but out of view for screen shares
  4. Quickly reference without interrupting the conversation

During Data Entry

  1. Use tooltips for quick field definitions
  2. Open side panel for more detailed explanations
  3. Check wiki for proper values and formats

For Training

  1. New team members: Start with the Media tab for training videos
  2. Browse plays to understand common scenarios
  3. Use wiki to learn your team's terminology
Note: The side panel content is shared across your organization. Everyone sees the same plays, wiki entries, and media.

Troubleshooting

Side Panel Not Appearing

  • Check that the Chrome extension is installed and enabled
  • Verify you're signed in to RevGuide
  • Try refreshing the HubSpot page
  • Look for the RevGuide icon in different areas of the toolbar

Content Not Loading

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify the content is enabled in the admin panel
  • Try closing and reopening the panel

Videos Not Playing

  • Check that the video URL is valid
  • Ensure the video isn't private or restricted
  • Try playing the video directly on its host site

Keyboard Shortcuts
