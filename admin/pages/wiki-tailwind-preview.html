Comma-separated additional trigger terms

The Lifecycle Stage property tracks where a contact is in your marketing and sales process. Common stages include:

  • Subscriber - Opted in to hear from you
  • Lead - Converted on your site
  • MQL - Marketing qualified lead
  • SQL - Sales qualified lead
  • Opportunity - Associated with a deal
  • Customer - Closed won deal
